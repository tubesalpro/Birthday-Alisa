Dearest Alisa

On this day you came into the world,
and the world has never quite been the same —
softer, warmer, more alive
simply because you are in it.

May your twenty-second year be a garden
where every dream you have ever whispered
to the night sky finally takes root
and blooms beyond what you imagined.

May the people around you love you
as genuinely as you deserve,
may every step feel lighter than the last,
and may your smile keep finding reasons to stay.

You are someone rare, Alisa —
the kind the universe takes its time making.
Keep shining. Keep being exactly
the beautiful soul that you are.

Happy 22nd Birthday, Alisa.
